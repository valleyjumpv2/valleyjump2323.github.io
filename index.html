About Game

Valley Jump is a Roleplay type Single Player Game developed and published by Team VJ Development, for Microsoft Windows. First teased under the codename Aspect in August 2021, the game began a closed beta period with limited access on September 10, 2021, followed by an official release on September 21, 2020
